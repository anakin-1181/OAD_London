Ledbury, The

London, United Kingdom

Book this restaurant on OAD Travel
Photo: Joanna Mroz
Photo: Joanna Mroz

Australian chef Brett Graham’s Notting Hill restaurant reopened in February 2022 after a brief pandemic hiatus. The new-look Ledbury is more than ever before an expression of Graham’s passion for produce. Beef and Iberian pork come from his own animals; venison from the deer parks he operates (Aynhoe Park and Boughton House); and mushrooms from the state-of-the-art, moisture-controlled cabinet at the restaurant itself. New dishes reveal themselves with the changing seasons – for example, Poole Bay mackerel, blood orange, myoga ginger and coriander capers or Jersey beef, white asparagus, XO morel and kosho sauce. The impressive global wine list leans Francophile.

Review by: Hillary Armstrong
